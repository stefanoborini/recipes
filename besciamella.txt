1 l Besciamella
---------------

- Butter 100 g 
- Flour 00 100 g 
- Salt 1 pinch 
- Nutmeg  
- Whole milk 1 l

Procedure
---------

- Warm up milk 
- Melt butter
- Add flour to butter, whisking. Put back on the heat to get it to brown a bit.
- Add warm milk  
- Add nutmeg, salt
- Cook for 5-6 min on reduced heat until thick in consistency.
